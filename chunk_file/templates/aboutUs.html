About Us

Chunkeet is a platform that accepts large CSV and JSON files and breaks them into smaller bits while maintaining the right format. The platform allows authenticated users, to upload their large files, chunk it into bits and download to their local device. The platform also provides each user with a dashboard, where users can easily access their storage, draft and also settings that helps them personalize their account on the website.
