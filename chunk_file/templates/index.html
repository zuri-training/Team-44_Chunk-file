What is Chunkeet

Chunkeet is a platform that accepts large CSV and JSON files and breaks them into smaller bits while maintaining the right format. The platform allows authenticated users, to upload their large files, chunk it into bits and download to their local device.

  1. Tap on the “Register” button to get started on your account or Log in to continue.
  2. Select your files and upload it or drag and drop into the “Upload file box” on your dashboard.
  3. Choose the number of header lines you want your file to be splitted.
  4. Click on the “Split File” button.
  5. Once your file has been splitted, you can “Download and Delete” or “Save for later”.
